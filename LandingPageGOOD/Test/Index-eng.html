We are IIAA

Since we believe in opportunities, we constitute ourselves in the context of the crisis generated by COVID-19, with the firm commitment to contribute to the civil aviation community international, providing integrated and innovative solutions for the development of professionals and organizations linked to the airline industry.

Our Founder

Mario Martínez, with more than 35 years of experience in the field of civil aviation in the areas of air traffic control and direction of aeronautical training, contributing to the development of the airline sector in El Salvador, Central America and the rest of Latin America ..

Mission & Vision

Mission: We provide organizations and professionals linked to aviation civil, inclusive, flexible and innovative solutions for training, technical assistance and development technological, generating value for our stakeholders, through the application of high standards and internationally recognized best practices.

Vision: To become a solid international benchmark in the provision of integrated solutions in the field of civil aviation.
